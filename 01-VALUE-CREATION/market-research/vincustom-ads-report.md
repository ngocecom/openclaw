## 💰 REVENUE MODEL ANALYSIS

### Free + S&H Model
- **Product:** FREE T-shirt
- **Customer Pays:** Shipping + Handling (~$9.95-14.95)
- **Upsell:** Additional items at checkout
- **Break-even:** ~500-1,000 orders/day needed

### Bundle Model
- **Offer:** Buy Hoodie (~$49.95) Get T-Shirt FREE
- **AOV:** ~$50-60 (higher than S&H only)
- **Margin:** Better than free model

### Paid Products
- **Custom Hats:** $29.95-39.95
